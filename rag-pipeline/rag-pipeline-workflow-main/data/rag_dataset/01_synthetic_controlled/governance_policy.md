# Governance Policy

## 1. Cau truc quan tri

Cong ty ap dung mo hinh quan tri gom:

- Dai hoi dong co dong.
- Hoi dong quan tri (HDQT).
- Ban Kiem soat.
- Ban dieu hanh.

## 2. Thanh phan HDQT (gia lap)

| Vai tro | So luong | Ghi chu |
|---|---|---|
| Thanh vien dieu hanh | 2 | Co CEO |
| Thanh vien khong dieu hanh | 3 | Doc lap 2 nguoi |
| Tong cong | 5 | Nhiem ky 3 nam |

## 3. Uy ban truc thuoc HDQT

- Uy ban Kiem toan va Rui ro.
- Uy ban Luong thuong va De cu.
- Uy ban Phat trien ben vung.

## 4. Nguyen tac dao duc kinh doanh

- Cam hoi lo va chi tra khong minh bach.
- Cong bo xung dot loi ich theo quy trinh.
- Qua tang doi tac co gia tri > 100 USD phai khai bao.

## 5. Kiem soat noi bo va rui ro

### Khung quan tri rui ro

- Nhan dien rui ro: hang quy.
- Danh gia xac suat/tac dong: theo thang 5 muc.
- Ke hoach giam thieu: owner va deadline ro rang.

### Chu ky bao cao

| Bao cao | Tan suat | Nhan |
|---|---|---|
| Risk register | Hang quy | Uy ban Kiem toan va Rui ro |
| Incident report | Trong 48 gio | CEO + Compliance |
| Internal audit summary | 2 lan/nam | HDQT |

## 6. Bao ve du lieu va an ninh thong tin

- Phan loai du lieu: Public, Internal, Confidential.
- Du lieu Confidential phai ma hoa khi luu tru va truyen.
- Su co an ninh thong tin muc nghiem trong bao cao trong 24 gio.
