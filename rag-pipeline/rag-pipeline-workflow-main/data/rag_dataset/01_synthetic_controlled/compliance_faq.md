# Compliance FAQ

## 1. Muc dich

Tai lieu hoi dap nhanh ve tuan thu noi bo, dao duc kinh doanh va co che bao cao vi pham.

## 2. Cau hoi thuong gap

### Q1: Khi nao can khai bao xung dot loi ich?

- Bat ky khi nao nhan vien hoac quan ly co loi ich tai chinh/quan he co the anh huong den quyet dinh cong viec.
- Khai bao qua form `Conflict of Interest Declaration` trong vong 7 ngay.

### Q2: Gioi han qua tang doi tac la bao nhieu?

- Nhan qua tang tri gia tren 100 USD phai khai bao va xin phe duyet cua Compliance.
- Tien mat hoac qua tang quy doi tien mat la khong duoc phep.

### Q3: Toi co the bao cao hanh vi vi pham o dau?

- Email: ethics@greenriver.example
- Hotline: 1800-555-ETHICS (24/7)
- Form an danh tren intranet.

### Q4: Cong ty co bao ve nguoi to cao khong?

- Co. Cong ty cam tra dua voi nguoi to cao dung quy trinh.
- Moi hanh vi tra dua se bi xu ly ky luat.

### Q5: Han xu ly phan hoi ban dau la bao lau?

- Trong vong 5 ngay lam viec.
