# Social Policy

## 1. Nguyen tac chung

Cong ty cam ket:

- Ton trong quyen con nguoi.
- Khong phan biet doi xu.
- Dam bao moi truong lam viec an toan, ton trong.

## 2. Chinh sach lao dong

### Tuyen dung va hop dong

- Khong su dung lao dong duoi 18 tuoi cho vi tri san xuat.
- 100% nhan vien co hop dong lao dong ro rang.

### Thu lao va phuc loi

- Muc luong toi thieu noi bo cao hon 12% so voi muc luong toi thieu vung.
- Bao hiem bat buoc va bao hiem suc khoe bo sung cho nhan vien toan thoi gian.

### Gio lam va nghi ngoi

- Gio lam chuan: 8 gio/ngay, 5.5 ngay/tuan.
- Lam them khong vuot 30 gio/thang, tru truong hop duoc phe duyet dac biet.

## 3. Da dang, cong bang va hoa nhap (DEI)

| Chi so DEI | Gia tri 2025 | Muc tieu 2027 |
|---|---|---|
| Ty le nu toan cong ty | 47% | 48% |
| Ty le nu cap quan ly trung | 34% | 40% |
| Ty le nhan vien khuyet tat | 1.2% | 2.0% |

## 4. Suc khoe va an toan lao dong

- LTIFR muc tieu <= 0.35 trong nam 2026.
- 100% nhan vien nha may phai hoan thanh dao tao EHS co ban.
- Dien tap PCCC toi thieu 2 lan/nam tai moi co so.

## 5. Co che khieu nai va to cao

- Co kenh to cao bao mat qua email va hotline.
- Cam tra dua nguoi to cao dung quy trinh.
- Phan hoi ban dau trong vong 5 ngay lam viec.
