# Product Internal FAQ

## 1. Pham vi

FAQ noi bo cho dong san pham gia dung thong minh GR-Home (du lieu gia lap).

## 2. Cau hoi thuong gap

### Q1: Bao hanh tieu chuan cua dong GR-Home la bao lau?

- Bao hanh tieu chuan: 24 thang ke tu ngay mua.
- Linh kien tieu hao khong nam trong pham vi bao hanh tieu chuan.

### Q2: Dieu kien doi tra trong 30 ngay la gi?

- San pham loi ky thuat do nha san xuat.
- Con du phu kien co ban.
- Co hoa don hop le.

### Q3: San pham co muc tieu tiet kiem nang luong nao?

- Dong GR-Home 2026 dat muc giam 18% dien tieu thu so voi the he 2023 trong dieu kien test noi bo.

### Q4: Chinh sach cap nhat firmware nhu the nao?

- Ban va cap nhat bao mat moi: toi da 30 ngay sau khi xac nhan loi.
- Ban va tinh nang moi: theo chu ky quy.

### Q5: Co ho tro thu hoi san pham khi co loi an toan khong?

- Co. Bo phan Quality va Compliance kich hoat quy trinh thu hoi cap do 1-3 tuy muc do.
