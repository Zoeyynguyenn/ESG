# Environment Policy

## 1. Muc tieu chinh sach

Chinh sach moi truong cua GreenRiver Manufacturing JSC dat muc tieu:

- Phong ngua o nhiem va giam tac dong moi truong.
- Tuan thu quy dinh phap ly hien hanh.
- Cai tien lien tuc hieu suat moi truong.

## 2. Pham vi ap dung

- Ap dung cho toan bo 2 nha may.
- Ap dung cho kho van va trung tam phan phoi.
- Ap dung cho nha thau tai co so (bao tri, ve sinh cong nghiep).

## 3. Cam ket van hanh

### Nguon luc va nang luong

- Uu tien thiet bi co hieu suat nang luong cao.
- Theo doi dien nang theo tung day chuyen.
- Dat muc giam tieu thu dien 3% moi nam.

### Nuoc va nuoc thai

- 100% nuoc thai duoc xu ly truoc khi xa thai.
- Tai su dung toi thieu 15% nuoc sau xu ly cho muc dich phu tro.

### Chat thai

- Phan loai chat thai tai nguon thanh 4 nhom:
  - tai che
  - huu co
  - thong thuong
  - nguy hai
- Cam van chuyen chat thai nguy hai boi don vi khong du giay phep.

## 4. Chi tieu bat buoc

| Chi tieu | Don vi | Muc 2026 |
|---|---|---|
| Cuong do dien nang | kWh/san pham | -3% so voi 2025 |
| Ty le tai che chat thai | % | >= 72 |
| Vi pham xa thai | vu | 0 |
| Ty le nuoc tai su dung | % | >= 15 |

## 5. Co che giam sat

- Bao cao moi truong noi bo hang thang.
- Danh gia tuan thu quy dinh hang quy.
- Danh gia ben thu ba moi nam 1 lan.

## 6. Xu ly vi pham

- Vi pham muc nghiem trong phai bao cao trong 24 gio.
- Bo phan Compliance lap bien ban, phan loai muc do va theo doi CAPA.
