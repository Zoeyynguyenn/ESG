# Design Intake Kit (for `/start create`)

Muc tieu: khi user chua co design, bootstrap van thu thap du thong tin de de xuat man hinh va tao mockup ban dau.

## Nguyen tac
- Hoi dung 1 cau moi luot.
- Cau hoi ngan, ro rang, uu tien ngon ngu tieng Viet.
- Khong hoi cong nghe o buoc design discovery.
- Sau moi 3-4 cau, tom tat ngan de user xac nhan huong.

## Nhanh nhanh check "da co design chua"
1. Ban da co design chua? (Figma/link/file anh/PDF wireframe)
2. Neu co: vui long gui duong dan/link va muc fidelity mong muon:
   - pixel-perfect
   - close-enough
   - chi lay layout/flow

## Neu chua co design: question bank (hoi tung cau)
1. Nguoi dung chinh la ai? (toi da 2 nhom)
2. 3 tac vu quan trong nhat ho can lam trong san pham la gi?
3. Man hinh dau tien sau khi dang nhap/mo app nen hien gi?
4. Co nhung module bat buoc nao? (dashboard, search, detail, form, report...)
5. Co yeu cau nhan dien thuong hieu khong? (mau sac, font, tone)
6. Uu tien thiet bi nao? (mobile, desktop, tablet)
7. Muc do accessibility mong muon? (co ban, AA, AAA)
8. Can da ngon ngu khong? Neu co, ngon ngu nao?
9. Co tham chieu UX/UI nao ban thich/khong thich?
10. Deadlines cho prototype/man hinh dau tien?

## Tong hop sau discovery
Sau khi du thong tin, agent phai tra:
1. Danh sach man hinh de xuat (screen inventory)
2. Luong chinh cho tung vai tro (main user flows)
3. Cac component dung chung can co (button, form, table, modal, nav...)
4. Ranh gioi pham vi v1 (in scope / out of scope)

## Mau output de xac nhan
- "Danh sach man hinh de xuat: ..."
- "Ban xac nhan danh sach nay chua? Neu thieu man nao, them ngay luc nay."
- "Neu ban dong y, toi se tao mockup/wireframe ban dau truoc khi vao coding."

## Khi user dong y tao mockup
- Tao wireframe truoc neu yeu cau chua ro visual.
- Tao UI mockup theo huong visual da chot.
- Luon kem mapping man hinh -> muc tieu nghiep vu.
